Fried Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • rice cooked
  • vegetarian chicken - chopped
  • peas
  • garlic - crushed
  • chillies - finely cut
  • shallots - finely cut
  • carrots - chopped
  • spring onions - sliced
  • egg
  • oyster sauce
  • fish sauce

    Steps

    1. add the sjalots, chillies and garlic to a hot pan on high fire
    2. fry untill the sjalots are starting to brown just a little bit
    3. add the vegetarian chicken and fry untill brown
    4. now add the carrots and fry for just a couple of minutes
    5. add the rice and fry for a bit
    6. slide the rice to the side of the pan and add the eggs, baking them
    7. once done, stir them in with the rice and add the peas
    8. leave on the fire for a couple of mintes while continously stirring
    9. now add the oyster sauce and fish sauce and the spring onions
    10. again fry for a couple of minutes
    11. serve with lime wedges
