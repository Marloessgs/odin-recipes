Lemongrass "Chicken" Stir Fry

Description

Ingredients

  • rice cooked
  • vegetarian minced meat
  • shallots, roughly chopped
  • garlic - crushed
  • lemongrass, finely chopped
  • dried chillies
  • chinese 5-spice
  • yellow bell pepper - diced
  • baby bok choy
  • fish sauce
  • lime juice
  • honey
  • lime wedges and lime juice

    Steps

    1. In an extra-large skillet, heat oil over medium-heat. Once hot add shallots, stir fry until tender, about 2-3 minutes
    2. Add garlic, lemongrass and dried chilies, stir frying until fragrant. Scoop this all into a bowl and set aside
    3. In the same pan add oil, increase the heat and add ground meat seasoning with salt and pepper until it browns a bit
    4. Add five spice, bell peppers and bok chok, lower heat and stir fry until bell peppers are just tender
    5. Add fish sauce, lime juice and honey misture along with the cooked shallots/garlice/lemongrass. Serve with lime wedges
